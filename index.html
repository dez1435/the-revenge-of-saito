The Revenge of Saito

A 2D Samurai Action Game

Story

Saito, the younger brother, lost everything. He saw his parents die, and his older brother Hattori—who raised and protected him—was killed by the ruthless warrior Musashi. Now, Saito walks the path of vengeance to finish what Hattori started.

Characters

  • Saito — The main character. A young samurai on a path of revenge.
  • Hattori — Saito’s older brother who was killed by Musashi.
  • Musashi — The powerful enemy who murdered Hattori.
